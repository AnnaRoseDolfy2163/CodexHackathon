> Built at Codex Nightline — OpenAI Build Week Kochi × KMRL  
> Solo project by Anna Rose Dolfy | College of Engineering, Kidangoor

---

## 💡 The Problem

Crores of eligible Indians never claim government schemes they 
qualify for — not because they don't need them, but because:

- Government websites are confusing and hard to navigate
- People don't know which schemes exist for them
- The application process feels overwhelming
- There's no simple, personalised guide

---

## ✅ The Solution

*SchemeSeva* is an AI-powered web app that asks you 5 simple 
questions and instantly shows you every central and state 
government scheme you qualify for — with exact steps to apply.

No login. No signup. No confusion. Just answers.

---

## 🎯 Who Is This For?

- Farmers who don't know about PM Kisan or Kisan Credit Card
- Students missing out on NSP scholarships
- Women unaware of Stand Up India or PMMVY benefits
- Senior citizens not receiving their pension entitlements
- Small business owners who need Mudra loans
- Anyone who feels government schemes are "not for them"
